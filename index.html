African Aquila Guest Lodge

is named after the majestic African Eagle, and is situated in the beautiful garden suburb of Walmer in Port Elizabeth. The guest house offers comfortable self-catering accommodation within close proximity to beaches, shops, beauty salons, and restaurants, an ideal getaway for both business and leisure travellers.

Accommodation is offered in 5 self-catering family units.

Each unit has its own private entrance, a bathroom, a fully fitted kitchenette, and a private BBQ / braai facility. The units have been tastefully decorated, and are equipped with microwaves, TV's with DStv hotel bouquet packages, and washing lines.

Guests are invited to relax and enjoy a lazy afternoon on their own private patios which are fitted with braai facilities and overlook the swimming pool and gardens. The cleaning of units and linen change is done on the day of arrival, and then every 4 days during your stay. Laundry can also be done on request.

We hope you enjoy your stay
